Everything You Need
Built for How Roofers
Actually Work

Not a generic CRM with a roofing skin. Every feature is designed for the roofing sales cycle — from knock to collect.

⚡
Storm Lead Gen
NOAA-powered storm detection auto-generates leads in your service area. See hail size, affected zips, and convert leads to jobs in one click.
📍
Canvassing Tool
GPS-tracked door knocking with script viewer, damage photos, and real-time map. Desktop dashboard with team leaderboard.
📊
11-Stage Pipeline
Lead → Inspection → Measurement → Proposal → Signed → Material → Production → Final → Invoiced → Collected. Drag-and-drop Kanban board.
🛡
Insurance Workflow
Full claim tracking with adjuster directory, supplement management, RCV/ACV/depreciation tracking, and timeline of every touchpoint.
📡
Two-Way Texting
Automated SMS at every pipeline stage. Customers can text back. Inspection reminders, approval notifications, and payment links — all automated.
📈
QuickBooks Sync
One-click OAuth connection. Contacts and invoices sync automatically. No double entry, no export/import headaches.
📏
Satellite Measurements
Order roof measurements powered by Google Solar API. Get total squares, pitch, segments, and imagery — all without climbing a ladder.
🏠
Customer Portal
PIN-based portal for homeowners. They can see job progress, view invoices, pay online, and submit service requests.
📄
Instant Estimator Widget
Embed a roof cost calculator on your website. Visitors enter their address, get an instant estimate, and become a lead in your CRM.
